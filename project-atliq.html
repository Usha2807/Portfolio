📊 Sales & Finance Reports – AtliQ Hardwares

AtliQ Hardwares, a global provider of computers and peripherals, faced the challenge of optimizing sales performance and improving net gross margins using a massive, unorganized sales dataset. This project aimed to transform that raw data into strategic business insights.

Using only Excel and Power Query, I performed extensive data cleaning and transformation (ETL) on over half a million records. The project resulted in the creation of comprehensive, stakeholder-ready reports that enhance AtliQ’s ability to track KPIs, assess profitability, and support informed decision-making across markets and fiscal periods.

🎯 Key Deliverables

  • Customer & Market Reports: Analyzed customer trends and market performance against targets
  • P&L Statements: Generated by fiscal year, month, and market for financial benchmarking
  • ETL via Power Query: Built structured data models with date tables and fiscal time logic
  • Stakeholder Readiness: Clean, intuitive reports optimized for strategic review and communication

🛠️ Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Power Query (ETL, custom fiscal calendars, transformations)
  • Power Pivot for data modeling

💡 Why It Matters

This project demonstrates how Excel and Power Query can deliver real business value even with minimal tools — enabling scalable, insightful reporting that supports AtliQ Hardwares' global strategy and growth.
